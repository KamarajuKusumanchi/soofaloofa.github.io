The incredible efficiency of the Pomodoro Technique

Posted on Friday, June 11, 2010.

I recently started using the Pomodore Technique and am absolutely amazed at home much my productivity has increased. After one full week I can honestly say I am a convert. My productivity, and my job satisfaction, has increased knowing that I am making progress towards a goal — each and every day.

If you are not aware of the Pomodoro Technique I will explain it briefly here. For more information, head over to the website.

The Pomodoro Technique can be split into five steps:

  1. Choose a task to be accomplished
  2. Set the Pomodoro to 25 minutes (the Pomodoro is the timer)
  3. Work on the task until the Pomodoro rings, then put a check on your sheet of paper
  4. Take a short break (5 minutes is OK)
  5. Every 4 Pomodoros take a longer break

That’s it, that’s all. It really doesn’t get any harder (or easier). Avoid interruptions and try to be consistent. Do this for a week and you will be amazed at how much you can accomplish.
